MyPath

Identifying Surface Characteristics for Generating Personalized Accessible Routes for Wheelchair Users through Participatory Sensing

Welcome to the MyPath project.
We aim to build an accessible routing and navigation system for wheelchair users to get to their destination from any source location. We achieve this by identifying surface types from user-collected vibrational patterns and subsequently use the surface type and incline information to generate end-to-end personalized accessible routes.
