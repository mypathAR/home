About MyPath Research

Empowering Inclusive Mobility Through AI

Our Mission

MyPath is an AI-powered accessibility project focused on transforming urban navigation for wheelchair users. We pioneer inclusive routing solutions by combining real-world data, user feedback, and ethical machine learning practices. Our mission is to create technology that empowers independence, enhances safety, and addresses the daily mobility challenges faced by people with disabilities.
